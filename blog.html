The Blog: Style Forensics

STYLEFORENSICS.COM, is a trendy pop culture fashion blog. StyleForensics was created by Edwin Exaus, a fashion journalism student at the University of Florida, to inspire, educate and motivate others who share a keen interest in the fashion world.

Users will be able to read what’s currently happening in fashion news, fashion designers to watch, new collections, style inspirations and an array of other things. Although the site is catered to men’s fashion, women’s fashion is spread sporadically throughout the blog.
